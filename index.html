The real problem

Most children do not need more nagging. They need a clearer system.

Many students forget homework, miss small deadlines, lose track of files, and only start revision when it is too late. Parents want to help, but constant reminders often become arguments. MyStudy Support turns daily school tasks into a simple shared dashboard.

What it tracks

Everything important, in one calm view.

Daily homework

Record homework with due dates, proof notes and completion time.

Deadlines

Keep tests, projects and submissions visible before they become urgent.

Schedule

Show school timetable, tuition, activities and packing reminders.

Revision Lab

Encourage short practice rounds and connect mistakes to review.

Mistake Book

Review mistakes until they are mastered, not just marked as done.

Parent visibility

Parents can see progress, proof and timestamps instead of asking repeatedly.

Current prototype

Built from a real student use case.

The first prototype was created for a Secondary 1 student to manage homework, WA3 assessment deadlines, school timetable, packing list, revision and study streaks.
